04 / 10
The product

Manifest in. Reconciled fleet out.

One manifest, three targets

Same YAML describes Incus, Docker, and bare-VPS placements. The driver layer hides the differences.

5-second reconcile loop

Idempotent. Append-only audit. Safe to interrupt and re-apply. Drift is a status, not a surprise.

OAuth rotation that doesn’t fail at 3 AM

Dependency-ordered, in-flight-task-aware, zero-overlap. 19 agents in 47 seconds.

Per-agent cost meter

$/day/agent and $/day/fleet are columns, not SQL queries. CFO-friendly export included.

The 14-line manifest

That’s it. That’s the API.

# fleet.yaml
apiVersion: openclaw.deploy/v1
fleet: prin7r-prod
agents:
  - name: nanoclaw-pool
    type: NanoClaw
    image: ghcr.io/openclaw/nanoclaw:1.4.2
    target: incus://dev2
    replicas: 5
    auth:
      claude_oauth: ref(vault://oauth/claude_main)
  - name: hermes-bridge
    type: Hermes
    image: ghcr.io/openclaw/hermes:0.9.1
    target: docker://144
    replicas: 2
